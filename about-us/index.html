About Us

 

HIRAYA INTERNATIONAL is a Non-Profit Organization, bridging Romania, France, and the Philippines through knowledge sharing, culture and arts participation, and sustainable development.

The objective of Hiraya International is to cultivate friendship and collaboration between Filipinos, French, and Romanians through cultural, artistic, gastronomic, and sporting events that highlight the uniqueness of each culture. It also aims to promote involvement in activities that supports sustainable development.
